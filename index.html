About Me

Hi, I'm Stereowalker, a developer passionate about creating immersive experiences for players and users. From game mods to fully-fledged indie games, I enjoy pushing boundaries and turning ideas into reality.

I specialize in medieval fantasy themes, dynamic systems, and ensuring seamless integration with existing platforms.

Current Projects

  • Reforged: A mod bringing realistic smithing mechanics to Minecraft.
  • Carnage Chase: A fast-paced racing game available on the Epic Games Store.
  • Two Twos: A minimalistic, strategic puzzle game.
