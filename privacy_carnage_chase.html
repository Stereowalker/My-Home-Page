Carnage Chase Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Stereowalker ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of our game Carnage Chase ("Game") on the Epic Games Store ("Platform").

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the Game:

  • Account Information: We receive your Epic Games account ID and username to verify your ownership of the Game and enable features such as leaderboards and achievements.
  • Device Information: We collect general information about your device, such as operating system, device model, and unique device identifier, to troubleshoot technical issues and improve the overall gaming experience.
  • Log Data: When you use the Game, we may collect log data such as your IP address, access times, and gameplay statistics. This information is used for internal purposes such as analyzing trends, troubleshooting, and maintaining the Game.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the Game: We use your information to verify your account, deliver the Game content and features to you, and personalize your gaming experience.
  • To analyze and improve the Game: We use your information to analyze gameplay trends, identify and fix bugs, and improve the overall gaming experience.
  • To communicate with you: We may use your information to send you updates about the Game, such as new features or content.

Sharing Your Information

We will not share your personal information with any third party except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate the Game, such as cloud storage providers or analytics providers. These service providers are obligated to keep your information confidential and secure.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if we are required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to comply with legal process, enforce our agreements, or protect the rights, property, or safety of ourselves or others.

Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as necessary to provide you with the Game and comply with our legal obligations. When your information is no longer needed, we will take steps to securely delete it.

Your Choices

You have certain choices regarding your information:

  • Accessing Your Information: You can access your Epic Games account information through your account settings on the Epic Games platform.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete the information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Additional Information

For more information about Epic Games' privacy practices, please see their Privacy Policy at https://www.epicgames.com/site/en-US/privacypolicy.

This Privacy Policy is designed to be compliant with applicable data privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
